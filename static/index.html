About

Chess Master 3000 is a chess game built for CSCI 402 that pits players against Maia, a neural network trained to play like a real human — not an optimal computer.

Unlike traditional engines that always find the best move, Maia was trained on millions of real Lichess games and mimics how players at specific ELO ratings actually think, including natural mistakes and tendencies.

Choose your difficulty from ELO 1100 (beginner) to ELO 1900 (advanced), pick your color, and see how you measure up.
